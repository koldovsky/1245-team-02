Still Have Questions?

Are you interested in one of our courses? Fill out the form, and we will contact you shortly. If you have any questions, contact us in any convenient way or see the section below.

Do you provide online or offline courses?

We offer both options. You can enrol in online courses from anywhere in the world. If you are in Berlin, you can attend offline classes in our cozy office.

What languages do you teach?

We currently offer courses in five languages: English, French, Spanish, Chinese, and Ukrainian.

Do you have individual lessons?

Yes, we offer one-on-one private lessons in the chosen language with a teacher. Contact us to find out more about available options, tutors, and prices for individual lessons.

What are intensive courses?

An intensive language course consists of 10-20 lessons, which take place 3-4 times a week. Thanks to this, you immerse yourself in the language and quickly master the basic knowledge.
